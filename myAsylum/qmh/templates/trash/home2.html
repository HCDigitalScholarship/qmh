About The Project

The Quakers & Mental Health portal launched in the summer of 2015 as an iterative portal to hold scholarship about the history of mental health in Philadelphia, in the 19th century, and in particular of Friends Hospital, the first private mental health institution in the United States. This multi-year project combines archival research and writing with digital scholarship to build the portal, to create visualizations, to analyze data from the archival research and to support scholarship on the history of mental health in Philadelphia.
